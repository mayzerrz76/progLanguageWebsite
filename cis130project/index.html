Choosing the Right Programming Language

Computer programming has made significant progress over the last several decades. In the past, computer programmers were few and far between, and were viewed with awe. We now live in a digital age, and an age of information, and many IT jobs now require a solid understanding of a programming language. Often times jobs require programmers to be proficient in more than one language. If you're new to programming, it may be a little overwhelming to decide which language to learn first. This site gives a broad background to several of the most popular programming languages out there.

Where Should I Start?

There are many considerations that may affect one's choice on which language to begin with. It is important to consider the dificulty level one is willing to learn at, skills you may already possess that may give you an edge, and your long term goal for learning the language.

Regardless of what your end goal is, you need to be careful to choose the right programming language to help you achieve said goal. This site will highlight some of the functionality and uses of today's most popular languages. It is important to remember that languages become more complicated as you become more proficient and experienced using a language.
